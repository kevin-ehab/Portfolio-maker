Project: Axon

Description:

It's a talking robot that helps children learn in a fun way. It can move its arms in a circular pattern to grab their attention and move around using its wheels. It's movement can be controlled remotely. It is energy efficient because it can be charged using solar energy or using a charger. It has LED lights in its eyes which entertain children even more! Its voice output is controlled by an app that is installed on your phone. Super cool, isn't it?
